Emoter/Dynamic font design

This project was inspired by my observations of human emotion and my own experience. In daily life, people may often hide their true feelings in front of others, this does not mean that mood is simple. I have done some research, it shows that many people admit they recall what happened recently or just feeling the moment when they are alone and discover more fine inner emotions than they are with others. So I tended to use the way of graphic design to express these emotions exaggerated and without restriction. To encourage people to pay more attention to their own heart under the big pressure of life nowadays.

These emotions are very abundant, including anger, desire, jealousy, loneliness, etc., and they all have unique "personality" and "appearance". So I used 26 alphabets to represent 26 emotions. According to their varied and abstract features, I made them become little monsters who does not exist in reality. I also made an short animation of them, so they can show the emotions both through their appearance and the animation.

Every alphabet has a name which is the word of its emotion, and the alphabet is the initial litter of the word. In addition, the alphabets all together is a family, its name is Emoter, which means emotion plus monster.
