Monkey bread/Packaging

Monkey bread is a kind of tropic plant which also can be called baobab.In the tropic areas, people and animals can get sufficient water from the trunks of baobabs to survive in the harsh climate.Monkey bread also shows up in the novel The Little Prince-it grows so fast that the planet has almost been seized by it.

The inspiration comes from my observation of the nature and human beings. Human and the nature are two symbols of vitality, but compared with the long-lasting nature, it is a pity that our life time is shorty. So I tried to combine these two elements to express the concept of immortal life.
