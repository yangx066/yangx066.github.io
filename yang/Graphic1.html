Emoter/Handmade book design

This project is an extension of Emoter. It includes 4 books in series.These they are used to describe the detail of my design process and the animation.

The first 2 books include six alphabets and seven alphabets respectively, also the other 2 books. Every single book has 8 pages(including the cover and back cover), and each page shows an alphabet.

I chose folding to the books and used cotton cloth to connect each page(made of 3mm hardboard), so that it will be kind and highlight the image of each alphabet. And I also used unweaven materials with bump colors to make the covers and the back covers which could cause a strong visual impact and express emotions of the little monsters intensively. The animations are also embodied in the books to help the readers understand my idea more clearly.

The right part of each book is a paper box with a drawer structure. The animation booklets and the specification of the booklets are in the boxes. The readers can see the dynamic of the alphabet by following the guidance of the specification.
